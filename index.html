Asaalamualaikum. Surah Al Fatiha (in Arabic text: الْفَاتِحَة‎) is Surah 1 or the 1st chapter of the Holy Qur'an. The English meaning of this Surah is called “The Opening”. It is classified as a Meccan Surah meaning it's revelation was before the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) had migrated to yathrib (medina).
